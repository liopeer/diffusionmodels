diffusion_models.losses.kl_divergence.gaussian_kl

diffusion_models.losses.kl_divergence.gaussian_kl(p_mean, p_var, q_mean, q_var)

Calculate KL Divergence of 2 Gaussian distributions.

KL divergence between two univariate Gaussians, as derived in [1], with k=1 (dimensionality).

Parameters:

  • p_mean (Float[Tensor, '1']) – mean value of first distribution

  • p_var (Float[Tensor, '1']) – variance value of first distribution

  • q_mean (Float[Tensor, '1']) – mean value of second distribution

  • q_var (Float[Tensor, '1']) – variance value of second distribution

Returns:

KL divergence of inputs

Return type:

out

References

[1]

https://mr-easy.github.io/2020-04-16-kl-divergence-between-2-gaussian-distributions/
